Maps

The Analytics & Finance team has built up the mapping capacity since 2015 to provide in-house mapping and visualization solution for the Ministry of Energy.
Our team has established our Geographic Information System (GIS) platform for hosting online web maps/Apps and offering spatial analytic solutions for the decision maker.
We have developed strong ability on:

  • developing customized maps
  • offering poster-size printouts
  • conducting comprehensive spatial analysis
  • delivering visual products for evidence-based policy making
  • Long-Term Energy Plan Engagement Story Map

    In fall and winter 2016-2017, the Ministry of Energy held an extensive engagement process to support development of Ontario's next Long-Term Energy Plan (LTEP).
    This Story Map provides an overview on the engagement process.


  • Boarder Public Sector (BPS) School Board Analysis

    The Green Energy Act mandates broader public sector (BPS) entities within Ontario to report their annual energy consumption. This study is focusing on School Board Sector to understand the energy use on school board level and building level. BI analytical tool is integrated in the product to provide quick insights.


  • Electric Vehicle in Ontario 2016

    The analysis is to understand the electric vehicle (EV) distribution in Ontario and to focus on how the local distribution companies (LDCs) are impacted by the increasing EV sales. The study utilizes electric vehicle incentive payment (EVIP) data provided by Ministry of Transportation on Sept 2016.
