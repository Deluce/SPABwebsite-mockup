Analytics

The Analytics & Finance unit provides analytics advice to various units within the Ministry of Energy.

A&F brings together expertise in data gathering and management, followed by designing high-quality data visualizations and dashboards that bring out policy insights from various data sources.

  • Energy Dashboard

    This Energy Dashboard gives an overview of the Ontario's Energy usage. It displays the Energy demand, supplies and price.


  • Strategy Policy and Analytics Projects

    The dashboard displays the important projects that we have finished.


  • Public comments about the Ministry of Energy’s Long-Term Energy Plan

    The dashboard summarizes the topics covered by roughly 1500 comments received from the public through the Environmental Registry during the development of the 2017 Long-Term Energy Plan, and rovides an at-a-glance overview of the attitudes of the public towards energy policy.


  • The Ministry of Energy’s Facebook news feed

    This dashboard summarizes activities on the Ministry of Energy’s Facebook newsfeed. The dashboard was created as a test of real-time text analytics.


  • Analysis on Hospitals

    This analysis summarizes the change in Ontario hospitals’ electricity costs over the past 5 years (as reported on by the Canadian Taxpayers Federation), and compares cost trends with hospitals’ electricity consumption.
